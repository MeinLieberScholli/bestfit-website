Frequently Asked Questions

Registration

What is the advantage of training with registration compared to without registration?

Without registration, if something happens to your smartphone (reset, lost), all your logged workout data will be lost forever.

Can I still register later on?

Yes, just open the settings in the app (bottom right) and click on "Registration" at the top.

Workout plan

Why do I always get a new workout plan after 3-4 weeks?

During the workout your central nervous system and muscles adapt. After 3-4 weeks a new training stimulus should be introduced through new exercises and training methods, so that the optimal adjustment is always guaranteed. Especially the central nervous system benefits from a change of exercises. It ensures a clean execution and the perfect control of the muscles. It also prevents stagnation in performance levels.

Why do I get different workout sessions?

We follow the supercompensation principle. The split is planned accordingly, so that the muscle stimulus is set at the optimal time. The split size depends heavily on your workout units per week. If you train more than once a week it is very likely that the split will increase. This prevents overtraining.

Can I restore an old workout plan?

No, your muscles get used to a training stimulus and should regularly experience a new stimulus to prevent stagnation.

How can I create a new workout plan?

1. After completion of a plan, you will be given the opportunity to create a new plan. 
2. Open the settings (bottom right), click on "Create new plan" under the heading "Training".

Can I have several workout plans at the same time?

No, you can only have one active workout plan, as soon as you create a new plan, you overwrite your current plan and cannot restore the old plan.

What is the information in the profile needed for (gender, age, height, weight)?

Based on this information we calculate the balance of the muscles in the later workout plan as well as the suggested weights for the exercises.

How do the different training goals change my workout plan?

The different training goals have an influence on the possible exercises, training methods, number of sets, repetition range and pause duration.

What happens if I mark a muscle as problem zone?

This muscle is given a higher weighting, which means that it receives more exercises and rises in order during training, which means it is trained earlier.

What influence does my information on training experience have on the workout plan?

The training experience has an influence on the possible exercises, training methods as well as on the weight suggestion.

Plan editing

How can I edit my workout plan?

You can replace exercises by clicking on the double arrows next to the exercise. You can add and delete exercises by clicking on the pen in the top right corner of the plan overview.

Can I create my own workout plan?

No, because our app is not a workout diary but a personal trainer. When creating the plan, we pay attention to the optimal muscle distribution (supercompensation etc.), the right number as well as selection of exercises, a demanding training method with a suitable number of sets and break time.

Replacing the exercise does not work, what can I do?

The problem should disappear when you log out and log back in to your account. Just open the settings and click on the last tab "Logout" (make sure you are already registered, otherwise your data will be lost!) Then log in again with your account and the problem should be solved.

I would like to mark a missing tool as available again, how does that work?

Under Settings, "Missing tools" you can mark tools as available again.

Can I add my own exercises?

Unfortunately no, because each exercise has to be assigned a number of parameters to function properly in the system.

Training

Why do I warm up my muscles using the equipment?

We follow the principle of a local warm-up. This means that you perform a warm-up set for each muscle group during the first exercise. This activates your metabolism, promotes blood circulation, improves the interaction with nerves, increases the reaction speed, joints become more elastic, cartilage can adapt to the load and the warm-up set brings your muscles to the optimal operating temperature. 

We do not rule out a general warm-up, e.g. running, cycling or crosstrainer.

The weight suggestion is not possible to adjust on my equipment, what can I do?

You can adjust the weight suggestion during your workout by clicking on the suggestion card. There you can set the increments for weight plates, dumbbells, equipment and cable pulley.

If the suggested weight is still not adjustable, try to get as close as possible to the weight suggestion. You should increase the weight to the next higher possibility at the latest with 1 or 2 repetitions above the given repetition range. Thereby you guarantee an optimal training stimulus.

What happens if I miss a workout?

The workout scheduled for that day is simply postponed to the next possible date, so it is not skipped.

What happens if I want to train earlier than planned?

The next workout day is simply moved forward, so no workout is lost and you do not have to train twice.

How can I cancel a workout I started by mistake?

Click on the "X" at the top left and then select "cancel". Do NOT click on "complete" as this will be considered a successful workout and the session will be closed and cannot be retrieved.

Can I retrieve a completed workout?

No, as soon as you finish your workout this session is finished and cannot be retrieved. If you want to cancel the training, you have to click on "cancel" instead.

Why does the suggested weight decrease although I have stayed in the repetition range?

The weight suggestion takes into account, among other things, the fatigue between exercises and sets, as well as your daily level, which can lead to your weight suggestion being adjusted even though you have remained within the repetition range. If you have done as many repetitions as you can barely manage properly, the adjusted weight suggestion will be correct.

Why do I have to input the number of repetitions completed and the weight used for each set?

This is the only way we can calculate a suitable weight suggestion that challenges you optimally and enables you to achieve fast results.

Where can I view my logged data outside of the workout?

Statistics → Muscle → Exercise → graphically as well as tabular

How many repetitions should I do?

Always do as many repetitions as you can just barely do cleanly. Our algorithm automatically adjusts the weight suggestion so that you train in the optimal repetition range in the next set.

Statistics

What does the light and dark line in the muscle overview stand for?

The light line represents the current percentage increase in muscle strength. The dark line represents the maximum increase in muscle power you have ever achieved. If there is no dark line, your current performance corresponds to the maximum increase.

What is shown during the strength development?

The strength development describes the percentage increase of your muscle strength over a certain period of time for a selected muscle. It is calculated on the basis of the performance of the completed exercises of the respective muscle.

What does the term 1RM, 5RM... stand for?

One Repetition Maximum (1RM) stands for the weight that you theoretically manage to lift once. 5RM = the weight you can lift five times, etc. The theoretical 1RM can be calculated from your normal training, so you don't have to risk injury to test your limit.

How can a 1RM also be calculated for body weight exercises?

For body weight exercises the body weight percentage which is moved by the muscle is calculated, from which a 1RM can be calculated.

Subscription

Do I have to get a subscription for every BestFit app?

No, the BestFit Plus subscription allows you to take full advantage of all our apps (Gym, Home & Go)

I have purchased a subscription for the Gym App, how can I use it for the Home App now?

The subscription is account bound, meaning it is linked to the email address you entered when you created your account with BestFit. 
You just have to login to the other app with the same email address and all your data and subscription will be automatically downloaded from the server. If you have an active subscription you can check your email address by opening the settings in the BestFit app and clicking on membership.

I already have a subscription and want to change my smartphone, what should I do?

The subscription is account bound, meaning it is linked to the email address you entered when you created your account at BestFit. 
On your new smartphone you just have to log in with this email address and all your data and the subscription will be automatically downloaded from the server. If you have an active subscription you can check your email address by opening the settings in the BestFit app and clicking on membership.

How can I cancel an existing subscription?

Open the Google Play Store, click on the menu in the upper left corner and select the "Subscriptions" tab. All your active subscriptions are listed here. Click on BestFit and then click unsubscribe.

I have subscribed but the app does not meet my expectations, how do I get my money back?

The entire payment process is handled by Google, so a refund must also be requested through them:
